Five BoC core measures on individual lines. CPI-trim is the primary read; toggle others to see where measures agree or diverge at turning points. CPI-common is the smoothest measure and tends to lag trim and median at inflection points.

CPI-trim and CPI-median are BoC staff measures published monthly. CPI-common is estimated via common-component extraction and is the smoothest of the three; it tends to lag turning points relative to trim and median. CPIX (ex-8 volatile + indirect taxes) and CPIXFET (ex-food, energy, indirect taxes) are older BoC measures, superseded by the current preferred trio but still published for continuity.
