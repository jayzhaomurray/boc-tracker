Statistics Canada Table 16-10-0004: capacity utilization rates, quarterly, seasonally adjusted. Total industry and manufacturing sub-index. Values above the historical mean indicate demand outpacing productive capacity; below-average readings point to economic slack. The BoC treats capacity pressure as a leading indicator of core inflation.
